Using convolutional neural networks and more hidden layers, we can detect and recognize considerably fast than traditional systems. In practically all recent major conditions, the accuracy for recognising the number plate from the vehicle was more than 90%. The system was able to detect the number plate for slanted plates with an inclination more than 60 in the video, but it requires more precision in eliminating unnecessary noises other than number plate pixels.
